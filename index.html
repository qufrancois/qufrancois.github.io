Personal Webpage of Quentin FRANCOIS

Université Paris-Dauphine PSL, CEREMADE & École Normale Supérieure, DMA.

I am a PhD student in Probability theory under the supervision of Djalil Chafaï and Pierre Tarrago. I am mostly interested in high dimensional phenomenas arising from random matrix theory and exactly solvable models.

Research Interests: Random Matrices, Free Probability, Integrable Probability and Algebraic Combinatorics.
